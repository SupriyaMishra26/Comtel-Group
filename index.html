About Comtel Group

Comtel Communications is a professionally managed company established in 2002, specializing in Premier Telecommunication/Office Automation, Electronic Security Systems & Biometrics Products, Innovative Fire Suppression Systems, and Logistics Management Systems.

With over 20 years of excellence in the Security & Surveillance Industry, Comtel has earned market leadership through our unique work culture devoted to customer delight and our core organizational values of Continuous Improvement, Teamwork, Commitment, Integrity, and Excellence.

Our strength lies in providing one-window service for all customer requirements, delivering total solutions with our commitment to achieve complete customer satisfaction - ON Time, Each Time. We are a 20+ strong team of professionals with presence across India.
